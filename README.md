## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive HR Analytics Power BI dashboard analyzing 
employee attrition patterns across 1,480 employees — 
identifying key factors driving employee turnover including 
salary, overtime, age, department, job role, and work-life 
balance to support data-driven HR decision making.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- Overall Attrition Rate: 16.08% (238 out of 1,480)
- Sales Department has highest attrition (20.67%)
- Sales Representatives face 39.29% attrition — highest role
- Young employees (18-25) leave most — 35.77% attrition
- Overtime workers are 3x more likely to leave (30.62% vs 10.36%)
- Low salary (Upto 5k) employees have 21.65% attrition
- Single employees leave more (25.37%) vs married (12.37%)
- Frequent business travelers have 24.73% attrition rate

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards — Total Employees, Attrition Count,
  Attrition Rate, Avg Age, Avg Income, Avg Tenure
- Attrition by Department (bar chart)
- Attrition by Job Role (ranked chart)
- Attrition by Age Group (distribution)
- Attrition by Salary Slab (income impact)
- Attrition by Overtime (Yes vs No)
- Attrition by Marital Status & Gender
- Attrition by Business Travel frequency
- Job Satisfaction & Work-Life Balance analysis
- Interactive slicers — Department, Gender, Age Group
